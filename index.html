Return Of Tyrone

The story beings with Tyrone. Tyrone is an African-American that lives in the slums of Harlem. You will have to guide Tyrone through gang violence and drug-deals, along with the racist cops of New York. Tyrones power-up items include baggy pants that fall down, gold chains, gold fillings, watermelons, KFC, etc. The object of the game is to get Crack. Tyrone is addicted to crack. The first mission is to find the legendary crack-pipe. Then, you must guide Tyrone through break-ins and burglaries so he can steal goods from homes to support his habit. But you have to avoid angry old African-Americans and the police. Hopefully, Tyrone can earn money to get some crack to smoke with those big lips. Watch out for this game, it's sure to be a big hit!!!
